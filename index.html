In 2010 our founders Adam and Anne fell in love with the location. The brick walls, concrete floors, and inviting interior were perfect for their vision of a community driven local cafe. We value our customers and aim to serve great food you can feel good about.

Committed To Community

Our goal here at Small Point is to create a better community by reducing our footprint in every aspect of the business. We source our ingredients from all over the great state of Rhode Island. We go out of our way to serve foods we feel good about. Eggs from Baffoni Farm, apples and cider sourced seasonally from Barden orchard, cheese, butter and yogurt from Cabot and Narragansett Creamery, organic coffee from Equal Exchange, and fresh local milk. We proudly offer gluten-free, vegetarian, vegan or raw options as well.
